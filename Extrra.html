Beagle


Weight:           10-11kg

Coat          Short haired.hard coat of medium length

Color          Many colors

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound,devekioed primarily for hunting hare (beagling).With a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent but single- minded. It is popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inhirited health problems.
